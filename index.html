About

I am a mathematics postgraduate with interests in Commutative Algebra,Homology,Category Theory,and Mathematical Visualization. Beyond pure mathematics, I enjoy programming,creative coding, and generative art as tools for exploration and experimentation. Much of my work focuses on visualizing mathematical ideas and building interactive systems that make abstract concepts easier to explore and understand.
